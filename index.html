The Five Gaps I Look For

Dormant Data

Useful information sitting in transcripts, forms, inboxes, payments, tickets, and customer behavior with no system reading it.

Disconnected Tools

Stripe, GoHighLevel, Fathom, Tally, email, calendar, and CRM tools working separately when they should be triggering action together.

Manual Repetition

Weekly tasks being done by memory instead of checklists, templates, triggers, or automation.

Unclear Decisions

Lead priority, follow-up timing, content choices, upsell moments, and reactivation rules living in someone's head instead of the system.

Revenue Leaks

Missed follow-ups, dead trials, forgotten free users, unpitched buyers, unreviewed refunds, and leads treated the same when they clearly are not.
